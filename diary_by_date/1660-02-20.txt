pamphlet, well-writ and directed to Gen. Monke in praise of the
form of Monarchy which was settled here before the Warrs. In the
evening Simons and I to the Coffee Clubb, where nothing to do.
After a small debate upon the Question whether learned or
unlearned subjects are the best, the club broke off very poorly, andI
_do not think they will meet any more. Hence with Vines &c to
Wills; and after a pot or two, home; and so to bed.
