1661-01-23

Ito the office all the morning. My wife and people at home
busy, to get things ready for tomorrow dinner. At noon, without
dinner, went into the City; and there meeting with Greatorex, we
went and drank a pot ofale. He told me that he was upona design to
go to Tenariffe to try experiments there. With him to Gresham
Colledge (where I never was before) and saw the manner of the
house, and find great company of persons of Honour there.'
Thence to my bookseller’s and for books; and to Stevens the

Patriarchae. So late to bed — having eat nothing today but a piece of
