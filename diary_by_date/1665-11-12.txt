1665-11-12

Lords day. Up, and invited by Capt. Cocke to dinner. So after
being ready, Iwent to him, and there he and] and Mr. Yard (one of
the Guiny Company) dined together, and very merry. Thence back
by water to Capt. Cockes, and there he and Ispenta great deal of the
evening, as we had done of the day, reading and discoursing over
part of Mr. Stillingfleete’s Origines Sacre, wherein many things are
very good — and some frivolous.
