1660-06-10

Whitsunday. Up and to my Lord’s. To Mr. Merstons, where
Monsieur Impertinent. At my father’s found my wife. After
dinner, my wife and I to walk in Lincolnes Inne walks. After
prayers she home and I to my Lord. Stayed there: and so to my
father’s, where I met Mr. Fairebrother. To bed with my wife.
