1663-11-26

Up and to the office, where we sat all the morning; and at
noon I to the Change and there met with Mr. Cutler the merchant,
who would needs have me home to his house by the Dutch church;
and there in an old but good house with his wife and mother, a
couple of plain old women, I dined; a good plain dinner, and his
discourse after dinner with me upon matters of the navy victuall-
ing, very good and worth my hearing. And so home to my office in
the afternoon, with my mind full of business; and there at it late,
