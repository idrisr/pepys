1663-10-28

Up and at my office all the morning; and at noon Mr. Creed
came to me and dined with me; and after dinner Capt. Murford
came to me and he and I discoursed highly upon his breach of
contract with us. After that, Mr. Creed and |abroad, Idoing several
errands; and with him at last to the great Coffee-house and there,
after some common discourse, we parted and Ihome, paying what
I owed at the Miter in my way; and at home, Sympson the Joyner
coming, he set up my press for my cloaks and other small things;
and so to my office a little, and to supper and to bed.
