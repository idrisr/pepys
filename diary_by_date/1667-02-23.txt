1667-02-23

This day I am by the blessing of God 34 years old — in very
good health and mind’s content, and in condition of estate much
beyond whatever my friends could expect of a child of theirs this
day 34 year. The Lord’s name be praised and may Ibe ever thankful
for it. Up betimes to the office, in order to my letter to the Duke of
York tomorrow. And then the office met and spent the greatest part
about this letter. At noon home to dinner and then to the office
again, very close at it all day till midnight, making an end and
writing fair this great letter and other things, to my full content — it
1. The Hague.
