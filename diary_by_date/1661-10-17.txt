1661-10-17

To the cook’s, and there dined with Capt. Lambert and had
much talk of Portugall from whence he is lately come, and he tells
me that it is a very poor dirty place —I mean the City and Court of
Lisbone. That the King is a very rude and simple fellow; and for
reviling of somebodyalittle while ago and calling of him cuckold,
was run into the cods with a sword, and had been killed had he not
told them that he was their king. That there is there no glass
