1662-12-14

Lords day. Lay with great content talking with my wife in bed;
and so up and to church. And then home and hada neat dinner by
ourselfs; and after dinner I walked to Whitehall and my Lord’s, and
up and down till Chappell time and then to the King’s chappell,
where Iheard the service; and so to my Lord’s, and there Mr. Howe
and Pagett (the counsellor, an old lover of Musique); we sang some
psalms of Mr. Lawes and played some Symphonys between till
night. .
