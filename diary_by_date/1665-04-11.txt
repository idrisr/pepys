1665-04-11

Up, and betimes to Ald. Cheverton to treat with him about
hemp, and so back to the office. At noon dined at the Sun behind the
Change, with Sir Edw. Deering and his brother and Comissioner
Pett, we having made a contract with Sir Edw. this day about
Timber. Thence to the office, where late very busy; but with some
trouble, have also some hopesofprofit too. So home to supper and
to bed.
