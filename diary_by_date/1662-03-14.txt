1662-03-14

At the office all the morning. At noon Sir W. Penn and I
making a bargaine with the workmen about his house. In which I
did see things not so well contracted for as I would have, and I was
vexed and made him so too, to see me so criticall in the agreement.
Home to dinner. In the afternoon came the German, Dr Kuffler, to
discourse with us about his Engine to blow up ships. We doubted
not [the] matter of fact, it being tried in Cromwells time, but the
