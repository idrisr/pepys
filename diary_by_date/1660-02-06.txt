1660-02-06

To Westminster and overtook Mr. Squibb and walked with
him thither; where we found the soldiers all set in the Palace yard to
make way for Gen. Monke to come to the House. At the Hall we
parted; and meeting Swan, he and I to the Swan and drank our
morning draught; so back again to the Hall, where I stood upon the
steps and saw Monke go by, he making observance to the judges as
he went along.
