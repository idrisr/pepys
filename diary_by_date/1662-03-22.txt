1662-03-22

At the office all the morning. At noon, Sir Wms both and I by
water down to the Lewes, Capt. Dekins his ship, a merchantman —
where we met the owners, Sir John Lewes and Ald. Lewes and
several other great merchants; among others, one Jefferys, a merry
man that is a fumbler; and he and I called brothers, and he made all
the mirth in the company. We had a very fine dinner, and all our
wifes’ healths with seven or nine guns apiece.
