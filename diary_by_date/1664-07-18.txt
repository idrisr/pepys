1664-07-18

Up, and walked to my Lord’s and there took my leave of him,
he seeming very friendly to me, in as serious a manner as ever in his
life — and I believe he is very confident of me. He sets out this
morning for Deale. Thence to St. James to the Duke and there did
our usual business. He discourses very freely ofa war with Holland,
to begin about winter; so that I believe we shall come to it.
