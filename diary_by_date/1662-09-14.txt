1662-09-14

Lords day. Sir George told me of a Chest of Drawers that was
given Sir W. Batten by Hughes the Ropemaker, whom he hath
since put out-of his employment, and now the fellow doth cry out
upon Sir Wm. for his Cabinet.
1. Created Duke of Monmouth, 14 February 1663, but known by that title for
some months before.
