office in the morning. This morning my Dining-room was
finished with greene Serge hanging and gilt leather, which is very
handsome. This morning Hacker and Axtell were hanged and
Quarterd, as the rest are. This night I sat up late to make up my
accounts ready against tomorrow for my Lord; andI find him to be
above 8ol in my debt, which is a good sight and I bless God for it.
